Mathematics Resources

YouTube

3Blue1Brown

Educational mathematics videos exploring a variety of ideas of varying complexity, ranging from calculus and linear algebra to topology and beyond.

KhanAcademy

If you haven’t heard about this one, then I have excellent news for you! they have in-depth, slow (read: almost tediously slow at times) educational videos on a wide variety of fields of study, including mathematics.

Mathologer

Fun videos about numbers and mathematics, sometimes they argue with Numberphile.

Numberphile

Fun videos about numbers and mathematics.

StandUpMaths

Primarily comedy videos featuring mathematics, along with general explorations of mathematics.
